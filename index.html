About Me

Prior to studying software, I started my professional career as a team lead in construction. As a team lead, it was often my responsibility to conduct hiring trials and train new team members. What made a candidate stand out to me in these trials, and going forward as a member of our team, was not the specialized knowledge they already had to their advantage, but their genuine willingness and passion towards learning and problem-solving. As I transition from building homes to building software, I believe that this attitude is my most valuable asset.

I've spent the last several months practicing critical thinking with a software focus, learning new libraries and programming principles along the way. In that same time I've deployed a few web applications using React.js with Redux for the front-end, and an Express.js or Flask server for the back-end. I used SCRUM methodologies to manage project timelines, and Git for version control. Head straight over to my GitHub or continue scrolling to check out these projects!
